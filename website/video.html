Taito F3 Video Signal

pixel clock: 6.6715 MHz
432 pixels per scanline (~15.44 KHz)
262 scanlines per frame (~58.94 Hz)

Notes

at startup, the f3 will output an invalid video signal (mostly consisting of garbled vblanks) for several frames.

scanlines 0-9 have two hsync pulses. scanlines 1-10 have shorter hsync pulses. (i.e. these mostly coincide, but not completely)

scanline 23 has one pixel of image output (most games hide this scanline).  scanline 255 is missing 1 pixel (some games hide this scanline)
