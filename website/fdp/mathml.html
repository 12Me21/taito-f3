The source row within the tilemap, for a given scanline y, can thus be calculated using: global + adjust[y] + ∫ ysync y zoom[n] dn

Where global is the playfield's Global Scroll Y, adjust is its Scroll Adjust Y values, zoom is its Zoom Y values, and ysync is the most recent scanline on which Scroll Sync was set (usually scanline 0).
Note that this assumes y ≥ ysync.
