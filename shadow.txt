onto pivot:
- mode2 = sets bit4
- mode3 = SETS bit12
onto sprite:
- mode2
 - at bpp=0: sets bit4
 - at bpp=1: sets bit5
 - at bpp=2: no effect
 - at bpp=3: sets bit6
- mode3 = CLEARS bit12


OTHER FINDINGS:
bpp=2 with sprites DOES have an effect unlike with tilemaps.
i believe it enables the bit5 bitplane only (it seems like bpp=3 is a combination of the two, as you'd expect)
ALSO, bpp setting does clear the low palette bits
palette 3, bpp=0 -> palette 3
palette 3, bpp=1 -> palette 2
palette 3, bpp=2 -> palette 1
palette 3, bpp=3 -> palette 0
